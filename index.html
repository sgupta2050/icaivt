About the Conference

The International Conference on AI-Enabled IoT and Vehicle Technology (ICAIVT-2027) is a premier global forum that brings together leading researchers, engineers, industry professionals, and academicians to explore the convergence of Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things, and Vehicle Technology.

This conference addresses the rapid transformation in intelligent transportation systems, connected vehicles, autonomous driving, smart cities, and sustainable mobility solutions through cutting-edge AI and IoT technologies.

Conference Objectives

  • Advance AI Integration: Explore the integration of machine learning, deep learning, and AI algorithms in IoT-enabled vehicle systems and intelligent transportation infrastructure.
  • Showcase Innovation: Present state-of-the-art research in autonomous vehicles, connected car technologies, vehicle-to-everything (V2X) communication, and smart mobility solutions.
  • Foster Interdisciplinary Collaboration: Bring together experts from computer science, electrical engineering, automotive engineering, and IoT domains to share knowledge and innovations.
  • Address Global Challenges: Discuss solutions for traffic management, road safety, environmental sustainability, energy efficiency in transportation, and smart city development.
  • Explore Future Technologies: Examine emerging trends in electric vehicles (EVs), battery management systems, charging infrastructure, autonomous driving, and intelligent traffic systems.

About Jaipur - The Pink City

Rajasthan's beautiful Pink City Jaipur was the stronghold of a clan of rulers whose three hill forts and series of palaces in the city are important attractions. Known as the Pink City because of the color of the stone used exclusively in the walled city, Jaipur's bazaars sell embroidered leather shoes, blue pottery, tie and dye scarves and other exotic wares.

Founded in AD 1727 by Sawai Jai Singh II, Jaipur is steeped in history and culture. Here the past comes alive in magnificent forts and palaces where once lived the maharajas. The bustling bazaars of Jaipur, famous for Rajasthani jewelry, fabric, and shoes, possess a timeless quality and are surely a treasure-trove for shoppers.

This fascinating city with its romantic charm takes you to an epoch of royalty and tradition. Jaipur has been laid according to the conventional nine-grid pattern that astrologers believe to be lucky and which has been recommended in ancient Indian treatise on architecture. Today, these walls may be more difficult to spot since the city has grown far beyond its original plan, but they are still there, proof that though Jaipur saw no great siege, it was more than adequately prepared for it.

About Poornima College of Engineering

Poornima College of Engineering (PCE) was established in 2000 with the aim of imparting pragmatic technical education. In its magnificent journey of 25 years, PCE has set various benchmarks and reached new pinnacles in Engineering Education with dedication, perseverance and devotion. PCE is celebrating its "Silver Jubilee Year" (2024-25) by implementing the academic autonomy conferred by UGC.

With student strength of 2500+ students studying in 10 specializations of engineering (CE, AI & DS, CSE (AI), CSE (Cyber Security), CE (R), ECE, EE, ME, Civil & IT), with more than 3.5 Lac square feet of built-up area, highly qualified faculties, state of the art infrastructure, good placements and industry-led curriculum, PCE is spearheading its outstanding voyage with motto 'Success is not a destination, it's a journey'.
